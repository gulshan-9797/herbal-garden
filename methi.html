🥗 Culinary Uses

  • Use methi leaves in curries and parathas for flavor and nutrition.
  • Sprout methi seeds for salads and detox drinks.
  • Use roasted seeds as spice or seasoning in Indian dishes.

💆‍♀️ Hair Care

  • Soak methi seeds overnight and grind to make a nourishing hair mask.
  • Mix methi paste with coconut oil to prevent dandruff.
  • Use methi water as a natural conditioner for soft hair.

🌿 Medicinal Uses

  • Drink methi water in the morning for digestion and sugar control.
  • Supports lactating mothers and improves hormonal balance.
  • Acts as a mild laxative and detoxifier.
