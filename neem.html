🌿 Skincare Applications

  • Apply neem leaf paste directly to acne, eczema, and skin infections for healing
  • Use neem oil mixed with carrier oil for moisturizing and treating skin conditions
  • Create neem face packs with turmeric and honey for glowing, clear skin
  • Use neem soap and shampoo for dandruff control and scalp health

🩺 Health & Wellness

  • Chew neem twigs daily for oral hygiene and prevention of gum diseases
  • Drink neem tea or juice to boost immunity and manage blood sugar levels
  • Use neem capsules or powder for internal detoxification and liver support
  • Apply neem oil to wounds and cuts for natural antiseptic protection

🐛 Natural Pest Control

  • Spray neem oil solution on plants to control pests without chemicals
  • Use neem leaves in stored grains to prevent insect infestation naturally
  • Apply neem oil to pet bedding to repel fleas and ticks safely
  • Burn dried neem leaves to repel mosquitoes and other flying insects
