  • Add fresh coriander leaves to curries, salads, and chutneys for aroma and taste.
  • Use dried seeds as spice or ground powder for seasoning.
  • Blend coriander leaves in smoothies for detoxifying freshness.

🌿 Medicinal Uses

  • Drink coriander seed water in the morning to regulate digestion.
  • Reduces inflammation and supports liver health.
  • Helps manage blood sugar and cholesterol naturally.

🏡 Home Remedies

  • Apply coriander paste on skin for cooling and anti-acne benefits.
  • Use coriander seed decoction to soothe eye irritation.
  • Chew raw leaves to freshen breath naturally.
